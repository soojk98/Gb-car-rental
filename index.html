Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to prepare?

To speed up your application, please prepare a copy of your NRIC (Identity Card), your Competent Driving License (CDL), and your E-hailing PSV License. If you do not have a PSV yet, let us know and we can guide you.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, a standard refundable deposit is required before you collect the vehicle. This protects both parties. Contact us to find out the exact deposit amount for your chosen car model.

Who pays for car maintenance and servicing?

We do! Routine servicing, oil changes, and normal wear-and-tear maintenance are 100% covered by GB Car Rental. Just bring the car to our panel workshop when it's due for service.
